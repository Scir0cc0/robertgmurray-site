How We Work Together

Engagement Model

Every engagement is structured around a specific outcome. These are the four formats that match most needs — select the one that fits your timeline and context.

Fractional Leadership
Fractional VP Product
Embedded product leadership operating at VP/SVP level — owning roadmap, leading the product org, and sitting in executive conversations — without the full-time commitment.
Best for: Growth-stage companies scaling their product function, or PE-backed firms between product leadership hires.

Structure: 2–3 days/week, 3–6 month minimum. Can transition to full-time if mutual fit is confirmed.
Advisory
Board & Strategic Advisory
Ongoing strategic counsel for founders, boards, and PE operating partners navigating product-market questions, technology decisions, or innovation portfolio management.
Best for: Boards wanting product-literate perspective on acquisitions, pivots, or platform bets. Founders needing a thinking partner with enterprise operating experience.

Structure: Monthly retainer. Includes availability for board preparation and strategic inflection events.
Diagnostic
90-Day Strategic Assessment
A structured diagnostic of a company's product function, technology posture, or innovation pipeline — with a concrete recommendation set and implementation roadmap delivered at close.
Best for: Post-acquisition integration, pre-raise preparation, or situations where leadership suspects product strategy and execution are misaligned but lacks a clear picture of where.

Deliverable: Written findings with prioritized action plan. Suitable for board presentation.
Project-Based
Defined-Scope Leadership
Focused execution on a specific product or innovation initiative — product launch, AI integration strategy, design system build, or market expansion — with defined scope and timeline.
Best for: Companies that have clear internal leadership but need senior capacity for a high-stakes initiative they cannot staff internally at the needed level.

Structure: Fixed scope and timeline agreed in advance. Milestone-based.
Not sure which model fits? Most conversations start the same way: a 30-minute call to understand your context and confirm whether there's a genuine fit worth exploring.
